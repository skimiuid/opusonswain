Studio Policy

Guidelines to ensure a productive musical journey.

Tuition & Rates

Tuition is billed monthly and is due by the first lesson of the month. We offer a flat monthly rate which reserves your specific weekly time slot.

Lesson Length Rate per Lesson
30 Minutes (Beginner) $45
45 Minutes (Intermediate) $65
60 Minutes (Advanced) $80

Cancellations & Make-ups

  • 24-Hour Notice: Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel a lesson. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice will be charged the full lesson fee.
  • Illness: If a student is ill, please do not come to the studio. We can arrange an online lesson via Zoom or send a recorded video assignment for that week.
  • Teacher Cancellation: If we must cancel a lesson due to professional engagements or illness, a full make-up credit or refund will be issued.

Materials & Instrument

  • Students are expected to purchase their own music books as assigned by the teacher.
  • An acoustic piano or a high-quality weighted digital piano (88 keys) is required for home practice. Keyboards without weighted keys are not suitable for our curriculum.
  • Students should aim to practice at least 5 days a week to see consistent progress.
